thor odinson

Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, the son of Odin and Gaea, the older brother of Loki, and the former husband of Sif. He is the current ruler of Asgard and the protector of Midgard. He is also the wielder of the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, which grants him the ability to fly and manipulate weather amongst his other superhuman attributes. Thor is one of the founding members of the Avengers and is considered to be one of Earth's mightiest heroes.
